Voxelarium

I designed the game's sound effects and implemented them using FMOD and C#. I created a variety of dynamic audio systems to make the world more immersive. One such system detects when the player is in a cave, and reacts by occluding outdoor ambience and applying reverb to the game's soundscape.

Redesigns

Clips from games and other media which I've used to practice my sound design skills

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

I captured this clip from my Nintendo Switch and recreated the audio using synthesis and recording techniques. The ambience for this clip was captured with my field recorder on Waiheke Island.

I was able to recreate the teleportation sound at the start by layering up synths. Synth was also used to create the wind while Link is paragliding, the swing of Link's sword, and the tree chopping sound. The sound of the tree falling is was made by scrunching up cardboard. The impact when it hits the ground was me dropping tree branches on the floor, pitched down and distorted. The paraglider sounds were created using a jacket.

Horizon Zero Dawn

A clip from the open world action-adventure, Horizon Zero Dawn, in which I've recreated the audio for the Focus and for the bow and arrow.

I ended up using about 15 layers to create the bow and arrow sounds. Some of these layers include: scrunching a leather jacket, pulling a zipper, flicking a ruler, swinging a broom, and punching a cushion.

Hyper Light Breaker Reveal Trailer

The trailer for the upcoming sequel to Hyper Light Drifter. I made heavy use of synthesis and audio effects to design the sci-fi weaponry sounds.

Eastward

A clip from indie studio Pixpii's action-adventure RPG, Eastward.

I recorded the squelching sounds of the slimes using a damp ball of toilet paper. I also made use of toilet paper to record the sound of the main character's footsteps.
