The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

I recreated the audio for this clip using synthesis and recording techniques. The ambience for this clip was captured with my field recorder on Waiheke Island.

I was able to recreate the teleportation sound at the start by layering up synths. Synth was also used to create the wind while Link is paragliding, the swing of Link's sword, and the tree chopping sound.

The paraglider sounds were created using my jacket.

The sound of the tree falling is actually cardboard being scrunched up with some effects added. The impact when it hits the ground was me dropping tree branches on the floor, pitched down and distorted.
