Ora has watched over the people for as many generations as they can remember. Never before has she been forced to become their home.

Help a lost community survive atop a colossal aquatic creature for long enough to find paradise. Plant polycultures to speed up growth, cycle crops as Ora migrates, and don't forget to prepare for when she swims to colder waters.


This was an entry for Regenerate Game Jam 2023, a game jam focused on sustainable and regenerative farming practices. The theme for this year was 'Seasons'.

The player is tasked with maintaining a farm on the back of a giant sea creature named Ora, and must maintain a steady supply of food, fibres, and flowers throughout an in-game year.

I wrote an evolving soundtrack to complement each of the game's four seasons, and also worked on sound design and programming.
