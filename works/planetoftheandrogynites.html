Bad Hagrid and the Planet of the Androgynites

Psychedelic Rock Album

This is the first released album by my band, Bad Hagrid. Set on an alien planet, it tells the stories of three characters and their relationships with one another, exploring themes of generosity and friendship.

Stylistically, the Planet of the Androgynites delves into progressive rock, psychedelic pop, and experimental ambient music.

The album is available on vinyl and CD.
