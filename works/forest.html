Forest

Spooky Musical Trees

This is my first attempt at making something using Three.js. I also made use of Tone.js for audio synthesis. Currently it works on mobile and desktop, but doesn't run on Firefox or Safari.

I was inspired by the phenomenon of trees communicating with each other through mycelium networks, and wanted to explore how this could be represented sonically and visually.

Click on the ground to plant some trees, and enjoy the music. :-)
