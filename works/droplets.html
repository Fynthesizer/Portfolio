Droplets is a physics based music sequencer programmed with Javascript and p5.js.

The user is able to place taps and bars by clicking on the screen. Taps produce droplets at regular intervals. The user is able to alter the interval at which each tap produces droplets by turning its handle. The droplets fall down the screen and will bounce off bars, causing the bar to produce a tone when they do so. The frequency and timbre of bars are adjustable by changing their length and width. Making slight changes to the environment can result in completely new musical sequences being generated.
