Reorientation Week

3D Puzzle Platformer

Reorientation Week is a 3D puzzle-platformer revolving around the mechanic of changing the direction of gravity. The player is in possession of a 'gravity gun' which they can fire at any surface to reorient gravity towards that direction. They must use this ability to make their way through a labyrinth of puzzles.

The soundtrack for Reorientation Week is adaptive towards the players actions, becoming more intense and layered each time they redirect gravity. The intention for writing the soundtrack this way was to create a sense tension as the world around the player is displaced from its natural state.

I recorded all the music and many of the sound effects for Reorientation Week using one synthesizer. The heavy usage of synths in the soundtrack is intended to emphasize the otherworldly, sci-fi atmosphere of the game.
