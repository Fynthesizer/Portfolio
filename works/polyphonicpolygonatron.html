Polyphonic Polygonatron

Music Visualiser

Polyphonic Polygonatron is a music visualiser programmed with Max for Live. The program takes audio and MIDI information from different tracks in Live and uses this data to generate three-dimensional shapes which represent the instruments.

I was inspired by the idea of giving visual form to the inherently abstract sound of synth instruments. Seeing the shapes react to the changing pitch and volume of different sounds, our minds naturally correlate the two.
