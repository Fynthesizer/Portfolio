Here Comes Haggy

Bad Hagrid Single

This is a single from my band, Bad Hagrid. As well as the titular track, a second song Kowabunga D00d is also featured on the single. The two songs were primarily written by myself, with the aim of exploring a more aggressive synth-punk style.

Here Comes Haggy is a twisted nursery rhyme about the mysterious, bearded character after whom our band is named.

Kowabunga D00d is a schizophrenia-inspired track about conspiracy theories which we in the band may or may not subscribe to.
